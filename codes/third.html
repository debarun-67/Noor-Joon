To yeh sirf aapke liye... 
Khayal ata hai kabhi hamein,
Chand kyon chamkta hai.
Kabhi tafakkur hota hai,
Ye sawan kis gham me barasta hai.
Ye wadiyan to sirf naam ki haseen hai,
Is aalam me to bas tu hi nazim hai.
Baad-e-rawaan talash me hai kamal ki,
Milti nahi hai tu, to misaal de kis jamaal ki.
Shab-e-tareekh mein jab zikr kisi naajneen ka hoga,
Sitaron ki mehfil me bhi tera husn bemisaal hoga.
Sawan se jab pucha ki gham kyun barasta hai,
Kahan usne, meri hogi jab tab hi theherne ka irada hai.
Jab tere husn se jalan khaye nizam-e-hasti,
Tab fitrat bhi bagawat pe utar ata hai.
Puchta hu us chand se, tujhe kis baat ka gham hai,
Kehta hai wo bhi, tere iffat ke age uska bhi noor kam hai.
Talab hai tu kayanaat ki,
Par ulfat hai tu meri.
Jaane kis husn-e-ittefaq tha,
Mohabbat se nawaza gaya main teri.
Teri hassen suhbat mein lemhe,
Mehke mehke se lagte hain.
Teri bas maujudgi bhi hame,
Jannat ke be-nazeer manzar dikhati hai.
Mulaqat hui tumse jyada waqt nahi beeta,
Par dil aise bandha jaise main raam tu sita.
Awal-e-dastaan hai yeh,
Khatam to sif yeh umr hoga.
Milunga agle janam me bhi,
Kahani phir waha se musalsal hoga.
Par baatein ye to baad ki hai,
Humein to abhi saath nibhana hai.
Agle janam me to phir milenge,
Par humein yahan bhi humsafar banna hai.
Yaad rahega woh pal,
Tumse wo kitni saari baaton kaa,
Wo jo baatein karte karte so jate the,
Un pal ke wo alag ehsasoon ka.
Jab pehli baar chuye the wo nazuk haath tumhare,
Laga ki jaise haathon mein afsara ho hamare.
Wo us din jab humse hooton mile tumhare,
Kaise likhoon sehr-e-awwal ko hamare.
Likh raaha yeh afsana tumko sunane ke liye,
Apne dil ki baatein tumhe bayaan karne ke liye.
Kehta to hu yeh sab mai tumko,
Na-jaane kitni kitni baar,
Phir bhi lagta kuch kam sa hai,
Tumse mohabbat jatana, dildaar.
Ab bas teri saari udaasi fanah kar dena hai,
Kheech lena hai zahar tera, aab-e-hayat bhar dena hai.
Saath mil jaye tera to,
Mujtama hum sambhal lenge.
Har mod, har mushkil ko,
Sahliyat se fateh kar lenge.
Itna prem ho jayega tumse,
Ye kabhi yakeen nahi tha.
Anjaan se zindagi ban jaoge tum,
Yeh kabhi socha nahi tha.
Ab to bas tum chahiye, tum chahiye,
Zindagi kaisi bhi ho, har dafa tum chaiye.
E naazneen hum tumse beinteha mohabbat karte hai,
Meri mohabbat-e-imam me bas tera haath chahte hai ...
